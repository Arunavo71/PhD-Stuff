
## Individual Shapley

+ As shown in [[Upsilon Values]], it is defined as: $$\mathsf{Shap}_i(\mu)=\frac{1}{n!}\sum_{S\subseteq N\setminus\{i\}}\frac{1}{n-1 \choose |S|}[\mu(S\cup{i})-\mu(S)].$$ for a game $(N,\mu)$.
